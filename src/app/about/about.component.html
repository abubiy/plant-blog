A bit about me


In 2004, I moved to the Driftless Area of the States from Ivano-Frankivsk, Ukraine. While finishing my Bachelor's of Science and Engineering degree at the University of Iowa, I relocated to the Chicagoland area to pursue a career as a software developer and business analyst.

I really enjoy nature and being outdoors! In my free time, you can typically find me hiking or kayaking on a nice day. When indoors, I'm working towards tailoring each piece of clothing in my closet (a never ending task when you're a shopaholic). It's also impossible for me to pass by a greenhouse or garden section without collecting a new type of succulent. I am a self proclaimed plant mom with over 15 varieties of plants scattered around my home.
